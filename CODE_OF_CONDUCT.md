# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on scientific rigor and intellectual honesty
- Welcome diverse perspectives and expertise
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project owners.
